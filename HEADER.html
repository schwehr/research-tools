Introduction

These chapters are being created for the 2011 Research Tools course. They are extremely rough. If you give them a read, I would appreciate any kind of feedback that you are willing to offer. The goal is to give students skills that will help them conquer data throughout their career. I am hoping to get this wrapped together as a book that people can take with them when they leave CCOM. I am releasing all course material under a creative commons non-commercial license, so that you can pass copies to your co-workers.
